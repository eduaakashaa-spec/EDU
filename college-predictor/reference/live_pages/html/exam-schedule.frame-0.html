Process & Streams

Exam Process & Stream Breakdown

1️⃣

Registration

Most exams open 2-3 months before. Keep Aadhaar, marksheets, photos ready. NTA requires DigiLocker for JEE Main.

2️⃣

Admit Card

Released 10-15 days before. Check all details. Design exams need portfolio & sketching practice.

3️⃣

Exam Day

JEE Main: 90Q +4/-1. NATA: Hybrid. NID DAT: Prelims+Studio. UCEED: Part A (CBT) + Part B (Drawing).

4️⃣

Results

NTA releases provisional keys. NID has no answer keys. Best score from multiple sessions counts.

5️⃣

Counselling

JoSAA for IITs/NITs. State counselling for KCET/MHT CET. NID/NIFT own process. NATA: 400+ colleges.

6️⃣

Reservation

Govt: OBC 27%, SC 15%, ST 7.5%, EWS 10%, PwD 5% horizontal. Design seats: NID ~425, NIFT ~5076.
