📌 IIIT Hyderabad — DASA 2026 Official Dates (Confirmed)

  • Exam Required: SAT (Math + EBRW) — minimum score 1070/1600. JEE NOT needed.
  • Super-scores accepted — IIIT-H takes best section scores across multiple SAT attempts.
  • SAT must be written on or after May 2024 (no older scores accepted).
  • SAT Institute Code: 6997 — report directly via College Board to IIIT-H.
  • Application Portal Opens: 23 March 2026 → admportal.iiit.ac.in
  • Application Portal Closes: 22 May 2026
  • SAT scores must reach IIIT-H by: 31 May 2026
  • Last recommended SAT date: 2 May 2026
  • Application Fee: INR 8,000 (non-refundable, paid online)
  • CIWG: ❌ Not available — IIIT-H does not participate in CIWG scheme
01

📝 Write the SAT Exam

Appear for SAT (Math + EBRW). Essay not required. Minimum 1070 to apply; competitive range for CSE is 1350+. Recommend 2 attempts — March and May 2026 SAT dates work well.

Last viable date: 2 May 2026
02

📤 Send SAT Score to IIIT-H via College Board

Login → College Board account → "Send Scores" → Enter Institute Code 6997. Request score delivery in advance so it arrives by 31 May 2026. Rush reports accepted.

Scores must reach IIIT-H: 31 May 2026
03

🌐 Apply on IIIT-H Portal (23 Mar – 22 May 2026)

Visit admportal.iiit.ac.in → Fill details → Upload Class 11 & 12 PCM marks → Set programme preferences (CSE / ECE / CND / CLD / CHD / CGD). No changes after submission.

Portal window: 23 Mar – 22 May 2026
04

💳 Pay INR 8,000 Application Fee

Pay non-refundable fee online through the portal during form submission. Net banking / debit / credit card accepted.

05

📋 Await Allotment → Accept Seat Promptly

IIIT-H ranks by total SAT score. If allotted, accept within the deadline window. Failure to accept forfeits your seat to the next candidate permanently.

06

🏛️ Report to IIIT Hyderabad Campus

Report to Gachibowli, Hyderabad with originals. Upload final Class 12 marks by deadline in offer letter. Pay remaining tuition + hostel registration.

📌 IIIT Bangalore — 2026 Confirmed Dates

  • Exam Required: JEE Main 2026 OR JEE Advanced 2026 rank — SAT is NOT accepted.
  • Process: Own independent counselling at iiitb.ac.in — NOT via dasanit.org.
  • Pre-Registration: Opened 25 February 2026 — apply now at iiitb.ac.in.
  • Full Application Opens: After JEE Main final results (~20 April 2026).
  • Last Date to Apply: 8 June 2026.
  • Registration Fee: INR 1,500.
  • Min Eligibility: 60% aggregate in Class 12 with Mathematics as a subject.
  • CIWG: ✅ Available — JEE Main rank required.
  • Programs: B.Tech (4-yr) & Integrated iMTech (5-yr) in CSE, ECE, DSAI.
01

📝 Write JEE Main 2026 (Session 1 & / or Session 2)

JEE Main by NTA: Session 1 (Jan 2026) and Session 2 (Apr 2–9, 2026). IIIT-B uses your best All India Rank. JEE Advanced rank also accepted. Target CRL rank within 15,000 for CSE/ECE.

Session 2 results: ~20 April 2026
02

📋 Pre-Register at iiitb.ac.in (Open Now)

Pre-registration is live since 25 Feb 2026 at iiitb.ac.in. Register now with basic details. Full application form opens after JEE Main results in April. Registration fee: INR 1,500 at full application stage.

Pre-register now → Last date: 8 June 2026
03

📄 Submit Full Application + Documents

After JEE results (~20 Apr): Upload Class 12 marksheet, JEE scorecard, passport/ID, photos. Choose programme preference. Last date: 8 June 2026.

Hard deadline: 8 June 2026
04

🎯 IIIT-B Counselling & Seat Allotment

IIIT-B runs its own independent counselling — NOT through JoSAA or dasanit.org. Multiple rounds based on JEE rank + programme preference. Accept allotted seat within deadline.

05

🏛️ Report to IIIT Bangalore Campus

Electronics City, Hosur Road, Bengaluru. Bring originals + pay semester fee + hostel registration + attend induction.

📌 DASA via dasanit.org — Covers IIIT-Delhi, IIIT-Allahabad, IIIT-Pune, IIIT-Sri City, IIIT-Kottayam + all NITs

  • Exam Required: JEE Main 2026 Paper-1 rank — SAT permanently discontinued since 2021.
  • Portal: dasanit.org (official — brochure not yet released).
  • Registration Opens: June 2026 (expected — after JoSAA counselling concludes).
  • Application Fee: USD 300 (non-refundable).
  • Min Eligibility: 75% in PCM (Class 12) for General; 65% for SC/ST.
  • Residency: Must have studied abroad ≥ 2 years (incl. Gr. 11 & 12) within the last 8 years.
  • CIWG: ✅ Sub-category for children of GCC-country workers — JEE Main rank still required.
  • Seat Allotment: 3 rounds in July–August 2026 (est.).
  • Coordinating Institute 2026: NIT Calicut (expected).
01

📝 Write JEE Main 2026

NTA conducts JEE Main in 2 sessions — Jan & Apr 2026. Best rank is used. SAT not accepted here. Target a strong rank — competition is high for top IIITs via DASA.

Session 2: Apr 2–9, 2026
02

🌐 Register on dasanit.org (June 2026)

Use JEE Main 2026 Application Number as login. Select category: NRI / OCI / PIO / Foreign National / CIWG. Verify eligibility — 75% PCM, 2 yrs study abroad, valid passport.

Registration: June 2026 (est.)
03

📄 Upload Documents

Class 10 & 12 marksheets, passport, JEE scorecard, NRI/OCI/PIO proof, photos. For CIWG: parent's Gulf residence visa + valid work permit + employer letter.

04

💳 Pay USD 300 Application Fee

Non-refundable fee via SWIFT transfer or international card. Keep payment receipt for upload. CIWG applicants pay same fee but enjoy lower tuition (INR-based).

Fee: USD 300 (non-refundable)
05

🎯 Fill & Lock Institute + Branch Preferences

Rank IIITs and branches carefully. Once locked (manually or auto at deadline), NO changes allowed. Research NIRF rankings and placement data before locking.

06

📋 3 Rounds of Seat Allotment (July–Aug 2026)

After each round: accept seat + pay advance fee within 48 hours or forfeit permanently. Results are only on dasanit.org — no emails or SMS. Check portal on allotment dates.

Round 1: July 2026 · Round 2: Late July · Round 3: Aug 2026
07

🏛️ Report to Allotted IIIT (Aug–Sep 2026)

Bring originals. Submit Class 12 passing certificate by 30 Sep 2026 if results were pending. Pay remaining fee + complete hostel, medical, induction formalities.
