IIT Admission — Full Clarity
Why are IIT ranks shown here? JOSAA publishes seat allotment data for all institutes (IIT+NIT+IIIT+GFTI) in one place. The ranks shown for IITs in JOSAA data are JEE Advanced CRL ranks. IIT admission requires: clearing JEE Main (top 2.5L) → appearing for JEE Advanced → clearing JEE Advanced → JOSAA counselling using JEE Advanced rank. JEE Main rank alone does NOT give IIT admission.

EligibleTop 2,50,000 (JEE Main)
IIT Seats~17,000 total
IIT Campuses23
OPEN Cutoff (approx)35–40% aggregate
Subject-wise cutoff~6–10% each

1️⃣ Clear JEE Main — get into top 2.5 lakh CRL

2️⃣ Register and appear for JEE Advanced

3️⃣ Clear JEE Advanced (separate rank list)

4️⃣ Register on JOSAA with JEE Advanced rank

5️⃣ Fill choices: IIT programs in order of preference

6️⃣ Seat allotted based on JEE Advanced rank

JOSAA Admission Process
1
JEE Main / JEE Advanced
Clear JEE Main (NIT/IIIT/GFTI) or JEE Main + JEE Advanced (IITs). JEE Main is January and April sessions.
Jan–Apr 2026
2
JOSAA Registration
Register on josaa.nic.in. Upload documents: 10th/12th marksheets, category certificate, photo, signature. One account per candidate.
Jun 2026
3
Choice Filling & Locking
Fill up to 25,000 institute-program preferences in order. Order matters — fill everything in your rank range!
Jun 2026
4
Round 1 Seat Allotment
Accept seat and pay fee (₹35,000 OPEN / ₹15,000 SC/ST) by midnight. Failure = seat cancelled.
Jun–Jul 2026
5
Rounds 2–6: Upgrades
Choose Float to upgrade automatically in each round. JOSAA 2025 had 6 rounds. Final seat after Round 6.
Jul 2026
6
Document Verification
Visit reporting center with originals + attested copies. Medical fitness test for some programs.
Jul–Aug 2026
7
CSAB Special Round
After JOSAA, CSAB fills vacant NIT/IIIT/GFTI seats. Separate registration. Good for last-minute upgrades.
Aug 2026
Float vs Freeze: Float = auto-upgrade each round. Freeze = stay put. Slide = same college, better branch. Always choose Float unless 100% satisfied.
Seat Acceptance Fee: Pay by midnight — no exceptions. Missing deadline = seat cancelled, no refund. Pay via net banking/UPI.
HS Quota: Home State quota for NITs has 15–45% lower cutoffs. Only your state NIT offers HS quota. Always fill it!
Supernumerary Seats: Female-only seats add ~20% extra capacity over regular seats. Girls with higher closing ranks can still qualify.
