All Admission Pathways at Amrita

Amrita offers multiple entrance exams and routes across engineering, life sciences, law, and more

🔧
ENGINEERING

AEEE — Engineering Entrance

Amrita Entrance Examination – Engineering for B.Tech admission across 8 campuses (Coimbatore, Bengaluru, Chennai, Amritapuri, Amaravati, Faridabad, Nagercoil, Haridwar).

  • CBT mode, 100 MCQs, 300 marks, 150 mins
  • 70% seats via AEEE, 30% via JEE Main
  • 19 B.Tech specializations offered
  • Two phases: Phase 1 (Jan-Feb) & Phase 2 (Apr)
🧬
LIFE SCIENCES

AEEL — Life Sciences, Agriculture & Medical

Amrita Entrance Examination – Life Sciences for Nursing, Pharmacy, Agriculture, Food Science & Allied Health Sciences.

  • CBT mode, 80 questions, 120 mins
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology & English
  • Conducted in 60+ cities across India
  • Programs: B.Sc Nursing, B.Pharm, B.Sc Agriculture
⚖️
LAW

A-CLAT — Law Admission Test

Amrita Common Law Admission Test for integrated 5-year B.A. LL.B. and B.B.A. LL.B. programmes at Coimbatore campus.

  • Also accepts CLAT / AILET scores
  • 60% marks in 10+2 required
  • Annual fee: ₹2,25,000 + ₹20,000 deposit
  • Amrita International School of Law
📊
ALTERNATE

JEE Main Route (B.Tech)

30% of B.Tech seats allocated via JEE Main percentile. Apply through the same AEEE portal — no additional fee.

  • Separate merit list from AEEE
  • Same CSAP counselling process
  • Can apply for both AEEE + JEE simultaneously
🌍
NRI / OCI

NRI / OCI / PIO Quota

Direct admission without entrance exam for foreign passport holders of Indian origin.

  • No AEEE required for OCI/PIO students
  • SAT accepted as alternative
  • Fee: ~USD 7,000/year for B.Tech
  • All 8 engineering campuses available
🏥
MEDICAL

NEET Route (MBBS/BDS/Nursing)

Medical, dental, and allied health admissions at Kochi & Faridabad campuses via NEET scores. Amrita Hospital is a premier super-specialty institution.

  • MBBS, BDS, B.Sc Nursing via NEET
  • Allied Health Sciences via AEEL
  • Kochi: 1300-bed hospital campus
  • Faridabad: 2000-bed hospital campus

Scholarships & Fee Concessions

Over 70% of B.Tech seats are in the scholarship category

🏆

Chancellor's Scholarship

100% tuition fee waiver for candidates scoring 80%+ marks. Available for top AEEE / JEE rank holders.

Renewal: Maintain 7.0+ CGPA, 75% attendance each semester.

📈

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% tuition waiver for candidates with 70-79% marks. Based on AEEE/JEE rank + Class 12 PCM performance.

One-time, renewable annually based on academic performance.

🤝

Need-Based & Reserved

Special scholarships for SC/ST, OBC-NCL, economically weaker sections, women candidates, and persons with disabilities.

Government schemes + university's own financial aid programs.

📊

Slab-Based Fee Relief

70% of total seats are in scholarship slabs. Top AEEE rankers (under 1000) can get up to 90% tuition waiver. Rank 1000-2000 may get ~50%.

🔄

Performance Upgrades

Students can move to lower fee slabs with consistent academic performance. Slab 1 needs 8.0 CGPA; Slab 2 needs 7.5 CGPA to maintain.

🌍

International Programs

Financial assistance for student exchange programs with universities in Germany, Russia, USA, Australia. Dual degree opportunities available.

Tips: Campus & Course Selection

Strategic advice from EduAakashaA experts for making the right choice

🏫 Campus Selection Tips

  • Consider your state: Local students (Tamil Nadu, Kerala) may get priority in certain rounds. Check if your home state aligns with a campus.
  • City vs Rural: Bengaluru/Chennai offer metro-city exposure; Coimbatore/Amritapuri offer serene, focused learning environments.
  • Branch availability: Not all branches are offered at every campus. Aerospace is only at Coimbatore. Verify before setting CSAP preferences.
  • Research opportunities: Coimbatore (flagship) and Amritapuri have stronger research infrastructure and international collaborations.
  • Hostel life: All campuses are residential. Hostels are well-maintained. Factor in climate preferences — Coimbatore is cooler; Chennai is warmer.

📚 Course Selection Tips

  • Follow your aptitude: Don't chase trends blindly. Use our Skills Assessment below to identify your fit.
  • CSE vs AI vs Data Science: CSE gives broad software skills; AI/DS is specialized. If unsure, CSE offers more flexibility.
  • Placement potential: CSE, ECE consistently see highest packages. But Mechanical, Civil have strong core-sector placements.
  • Long-term career: Think 10+ years ahead. Emerging fields like Cybersecurity, AI, Quantum Computing are growing rapidly.
  • Fee vs ROI: A higher scholarship slab (lower fee) in a slightly less popular branch might offer better long-term ROI than paying full fee for CSE.
  • Talk to alumni: Reach out to current students and alumni through LinkedIn or campus visits before finalizing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Covering the most common queries — with special focus on NRI students

🌍 NRICan NRI students get direct admission without AEEE?
+
Yes. OCI/PIO students and Indian students who completed Class 12 abroad with NRI-status parents are exempt from AEEE and can apply directly through the NRI quota at any of the 8 engineering campuses.
🌍 NRIWhat is the NRI fee structure for B.Tech?
+
NRI tuition fee is approximately USD 7,000 per year. Hostel and mess charges are additional. Payment can be made via international wire transfer or demand draft.
🌍 NRIDo NRI students need SAT scores?
+
SAT scores can be used as an alternative admission route. However, NRI/OCI/PIO status itself qualifies for direct admission without any entrance exam.
🌍 NRIWhat documents are needed for NRI quota?
+
Valid passport, OCI/PIO card, Class 12 mark sheets, parent's NRI status proof, passport-size photos, and address proof. All documents must be attested.
🌍 NRIAre NRI students eligible for scholarships?
+
NRI scholarship programs are separate from AEEE-based scholarships. Contact the Directorate of Admissions at Amrita or email contact@eduaakashaa.com for guidance.
🌍 NRICan NRI students choose any campus?
+
Yes, NRI students can opt for any of the 8 campuses — Coimbatore, Bengaluru, Chennai, Amritapuri, Amaravati, Faridabad, Nagercoil, and Haridwar.
📋 GeneralWhat is CSAP counselling?
+
CSAP (Centralized Seat Allotment Process) is held online after results. Register with ₹5,000, fill campus and branch preferences, seats allocated based on rank across multiple rounds.
📋 GeneralIs it mandatory to attend both Phase 1 and Phase 2?
+
No. You can attend either or both. Your best score across phases is considered for ranking. Phase 2 costs an additional ₹600.
📝 ExamCan I apply through both AEEE and JEE Main?
+
Yes. Register for both during the AEEE application — no additional fee. 70% seats via AEEE, 30% via JEE Main. Separate merit lists are prepared.
💰 FeesHow are scholarships maintained after admission?
+
Slab 1 requires ≥8.0 CGPA; Slab 2 requires ≥7.5 CGPA. If CGPA drops, you move to a higher fee slab. No disciplinary issues should be on record.
📋 GeneralWhat happens if I don't get a seat in CSAP?
+
If you don't receive any preference in the last CSAP round, your ₹5,000 registration fee is refunded. You may also contact admissions for non-scholarship/institutional seats.
📋 GeneralIs Amrita recognized and accredited?
+
Yes. NAAC A++ (highest grade), UGC recognized, Institute of Eminence by Govt. of India, NIRF #8 University, QS World 1001-1200, AACSB accredited (Business School).
📝 ExamWhat is the new exam pattern change for 2026?
+
A new Quantitative Aptitude section (10 questions, 30 marks) has been added. Physics reduced from 30 to 25 questions, Chemistry from 25 to 20. Total remains 100 questions, 300 marks.
💰 FeesWhat is the non-scholarship fee for B.Tech?
+
Non-scholarship (institutional quota) fee is approximately ₹6,00,000 per year. These seats are based on Class 12 PCM marks without entrance exam. Not donation-based.
📝 ExamWhat are the AEEL exam details?
+
AEEL is a 2-hour CBT with 80 questions covering Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English. For admission to Nursing, Pharmacy, Agriculture and Allied Health programs.
💰 FeesAre education loans available for Amrita?
+
Yes. Amrita has partnerships with banks like Bank of Baroda and Dhanalakshmi Bank for education loans. Installment payment options are available through the university portal.
