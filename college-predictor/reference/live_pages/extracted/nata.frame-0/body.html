NATA 2026 Exam Process

The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) is conducted by the Council of Architecture (CoA).

1

Online Registration

Register at nata.in with email and mobile. Upload photo & signature in JPEG. Pay fees online (General: ₹1750, SC/ST: ₹1250).

2

Eligibility Check

10+2 with Physics & Maths (50% aggregate) or 10+3 Diploma with Mathematics. Min age: 17 by July 31, 2026. No upper age limit.

3

Exam Pattern

3-hour exam, 200 marks. Part A: Drawing & Composition (offline, 80 marks). Part B: MCQ/NCQ (online, 120 marks). No negative marking.

4

Attempt & Score

Up to 3 attempts in Phase 1. Best score is considered. Qualifying cutoff ~60/200. Top colleges need 120-145+. Results within 7-10 days.

5

Admit Card

Download from nata.in. Available Tuesday for Friday exams, Wednesday for Saturday exams. Carry printed copy + valid photo ID.

6

Counselling & Admission

Colleges conduct their own counselling. Apply to 450+ CoA-recognized institutes across India. Meet individual college eligibility.

HEAD TO HEAD
Architecture (NATA) vs Engineering

Key differences to help you make an informed career decision.

🏛

B.Arch (Architecture via NATA)

✓Duration: 5 years (10 semesters)
✓Exam: Aptitude + Drawing + Creativity
✓Focus: Design thinking, spatial visualization, aesthetics
✓Subjects: Design, Architecture History, Building Tech, Sustainability
✓Careers: Architect, Urban Planner, Interior Designer, Landscape Architect
✓Salary: ₹4-8 LPA (top colleges up to ₹70 LPA)
✓Best for: Creative thinkers who love sketching & design
✓Regulation: Council of Architecture (CoA)
⚙️

B.Tech / B.E. (Engineering)

✓Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
✓Exam: JEE Main / State CETs (MCQ analytical)
✓Focus: Mathematics, physics, technical problem-solving
✓Subjects: Varies — CS, Mechanical, ECE, Civil, etc.
✓Careers: Software Engineer, Data Scientist, Mechanical Engineer
✓Salary: ₹4-12 LPA (top IITs/NITs much higher)
✓Best for: Analytical minds who enjoy math, science & tech
✓Regulation: AICTE — multiple specialization options

DECISION MAKING
Key Insights for Students & Parents

Critical factors to consider before choosing your path.

🎯

Follow Aptitude, Not Trends

A student who excels in drawing but dislikes JEE math is naturally suited for Architecture. Don't force-fit into popular branches.

💰

ROI & Salary Comparison

Engineering has wider starting salary range, but Architecture from NIRF top 10 colleges offers strong and comparable ROI.

📚

5 Years vs 4 Years

B.Arch takes 5 years vs B.Tech's 4. The extra year includes internship and thesis, giving real-world experience before graduation.

🌎

Global Opportunities

Architecture grads can pursue RIBA/AIA certifications. Engineering offers wider international markets. Both have strong abroad prospects.

🚀

Emerging Crossover Fields

Computational design, sustainable architecture, smart cities, and BIM blend both Architecture and Engineering skills beautifully.

💡

Practical Test: Try Both

Sketch a room design (Architecture test). Solve a math puzzle (Engineering test). Whichever excites you more reveals your true calling.
