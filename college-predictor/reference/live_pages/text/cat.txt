UNDERSTANDING THE EXAM
CAT Exam Process Explained

The Common Admission Test is a national-level entrance exam conducted by IIMs on rotation for MBA admissions.

📋
Registration

Register at iimcat.ac.in with personal and academic details.

📝
Application

Select 5 test cities. Fee: Rs 1200 Gen / Rs 600 SC/ST.

🎫
Admit Card

Download from portal with centre and slot details.

💻
CBT Exam

120 min, 3 sections, 66 questions, 198 marks.

📈
Results

December release. Sectional + overall percentile.

🎤
WAT/PI/GD

IIMs shortlist for interview rounds.

✅
Selection

Composite: CAT + academics + work exp + diversity.

🎓
Admission

Accept offer and begin your MBA journey!

Exam Pattern
SECTION	Qs	MCQ	TITA	Time	Marks
VARC	24	21	3	40 min	72
DILR	20	14	6	40 min	60
QA	22	14	8	40 min	66
Total	66	49	17	120 min	198

+3 correct MCQ, -1 wrong MCQ, No negative for TITA. PwD candidates get extra time.

INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS
NRI and Overseas Guide

Everything international candidates need to know.

1
Eligibility

50% graduation. Foreign degrees need AIU equivalence.

2
CAT or GMAT

CAT in India only. GMAT worldwide (650-720+).

3
Apply

Submit with passport, visa, NRI certificate.

4
Interview

Virtual WAT/PI. Overseas in Dubai/Singapore.

5
Enroll

NRI fees higher. Wire transfer accepted.

Key Differences
Fees: Rs 25-35 LPA (higher)
Exam: CAT (India) or GMAT
Visa: Student visa required
Scholarship: Merit-based available
Quota: 5-15% NRI seats
IIMs Accepting GMAT
IIM-A PGPX: 1-yr MBA
IIM-B EPGP: Executive PG
IIM-C PGPEX: 1-yr exec
ISB Hyderabad: PGP 1-yr
Score: 650-720+
