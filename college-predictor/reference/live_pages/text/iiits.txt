📌 IIIT Hyderabad — DASA 2026 Official Dates (Confirmed)
Exam Required: SAT (Math + EBRW) — minimum score 1070/1600. JEE NOT needed.
Super-scores accepted — IIIT-H takes best section scores across multiple SAT attempts.
SAT must be written on or after May 2024 (no older scores accepted).
SAT Institute Code: 6997 — report directly via College Board to IIIT-H.
Application Portal Opens: 23 March 2026 → admportal.iiit.ac.in
Application Portal Closes: 22 May 2026
SAT scores must reach IIIT-H by: 31 May 2026
Last recommended SAT date: 2 May 2026
Application Fee: INR 8,000 (non-refundable, paid online)
CIWG: ❌ Not available — IIIT-H does not participate in CIWG scheme
01
📝 Write the SAT Exam

Appear for SAT (Math + EBRW). Essay not required. Minimum 1070 to apply; competitive range for CSE is 1350+. Recommend 2 attempts — March and May 2026 SAT dates work well.

Last viable date: 2 May 2026
02
📤 Send SAT Score to IIIT-H via College Board

Login → College Board account → "Send Scores" → Enter Institute Code 6997. Request score delivery in advance so it arrives by 31 May 2026. Rush reports accepted.

Scores must reach IIIT-H: 31 May 2026
03
🌐 Apply on IIIT-H Portal (23 Mar – 22 May 2026)

Visit admportal.iiit.ac.in → Fill details → Upload Class 11 & 12 PCM marks → Set programme preferences (CSE / ECE / CND / CLD / CHD / CGD). No changes after submission.

Portal window: 23 Mar – 22 May 2026
04
💳 Pay INR 8,000 Application Fee

Pay non-refundable fee online through the portal during form submission. Net banking / debit / credit card accepted.

05
📋 Await Allotment → Accept Seat Promptly

IIIT-H ranks by total SAT score. If allotted, accept within the deadline window. Failure to accept forfeits your seat to the next candidate permanently.

06
🏛️ Report to IIIT Hyderabad Campus

Report to Gachibowli, Hyderabad with originals. Upload final Class 12 marks by deadline in offer letter. Pay remaining tuition + hostel registration.

ugadmissions@iiit.ac.in | +91-40-6653-1250
