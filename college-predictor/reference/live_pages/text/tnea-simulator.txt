🎓 What is TNEA Counseling?

TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) is the centralized counseling for B.E./B.Tech in TN colleges. Your cutoff (Maths + Physics/2 + Chemistry/2 = max 200) determines your rank and eligibility.
