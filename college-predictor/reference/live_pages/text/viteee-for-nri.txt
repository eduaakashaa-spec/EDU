NRI ADMISSION PROCESS
How NRI Admissions Work at VIT

The NRI admission route is fundamentally different — and more favourable — than the regular VITEEE route.

📋
No VITEEE Needed
NRI quota students are selected based on Class 10 + 12 academic merit — not VITEEE rank. This is a massive strategic advantage.
📊
Separate Merit List
NRI applicants compete only among NRI students — a much smaller pool vs 2L+ VITEEE takers. Better odds for better branches.
💰
Fee: USD 3,000–5,000/yr
Higher than regular (~₹2L/yr), but the ROI is excellent — VIT CSE grads average ₹7.5L+, recovering the premium in ~18 months.
🎯
Any Branch at Any Campus
With 80%+ in Class 12, NRI students can access CSE at VIT Vellore — the same branch that requires rank <5,000 in VITEEE.
🌐
SAT Score Boost
Strong SAT scores (1350+) can be submitted to strengthen NRI applications and potentially unlock merit scholarships at VIT.
📄
Required Documents
Passport, NRI proof (visa/PR/OCI), Class 10 & 12 certificates, photographs, income proof. EduAakashaA assists with complete documentation.
