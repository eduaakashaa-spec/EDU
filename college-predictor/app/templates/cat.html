CAT Exam Process Explained

1
Registration
Register at iimcat.ac.in with personal & academic details.
2
Application
Select 5 test cities. Fee: ₹1,200 (Gen) / ₹600 (SC/ST).
3
Admit Card
Download from portal with centre & slot details.
4
CBT Exam
120 minutes · 3 sections · 66 questions · 198 marks.
5
Results
Declared in December. Sectional + overall percentile.
6
WAT / PI / GD
IIMs shortlist for written analysis, personal interview, group discussion.
7
Selection
Composite = CAT + academics + work experience + diversity.
8
Admission
Accept offer and begin your MBA journey.

NRI / OVERSEAS

International Applicant Guide

1
Eligibility
50% in graduation. Foreign degrees need AIU equivalence.
2
CAT or GMAT
CAT conducted only in India. GMAT (650–720+) worldwide.
3
Apply
Submit with passport, visa, NRI certificate.
4
Interview
Virtual WAT/PI. Overseas centres in Dubai / Singapore.
5
Enroll
NRI fees higher. Wire transfer accepted. 5–15% seats reserved.
  • IIMs accepting GMAT: IIM-A PGPX, IIM-B EPGP, IIM-C PGPEX, ISB Hyderabad PGP
  • Typical NRI fees: ₹25–35 LPA (higher than domestic)
  • Student visa required before enrolment
