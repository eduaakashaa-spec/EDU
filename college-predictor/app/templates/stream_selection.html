DECISION FACTORS

What should drive your stream choice?

  • Aptitude: Logical & numerical for PCM · Biological for PCB · Analytical for Commerce · Verbal for Humanities.
  • Interest: Where do you lose track of time? Coding · biology · current affairs · design?
  • Career ecosystem: Each stream has its own competitive exams (JEE · NEET · CA · CLAT · CUET).
  • Higher studies: Some careers (medicine, architecture) require very specific school streams.
  • Financial & time horizon: Medicine & CA need long commitments. Commerce & Humanities have faster paths.
