STEPS

9-Stage Assessment Flow

1
Student Profile
Basic information · board · scores · category · home state.

RIASEC Interest Inventory
Holland-style test across Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, Conventional codes.
3
Skills Snapshot
Self-rating across maths, coding, design, communication, leadership.
4
SWOT
Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats — candid reflection.
5
Branch Preferences
Rank CSE, ECE, ME, CE, CH, MC and other branches by interest.
6
10-Year Vision

7
Parent Inputs

8
Analysis
Algorithmic fit-score across 50+ engineering programs.
9
Final Report
Personalised recommendation · branch ranking · top colleges · JOSAA choice order suggestion.
