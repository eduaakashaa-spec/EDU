IIT Admission - Full Clarity
Why are IIT ranks shown here? JOSAA publishes all data (IIT+NIT+IIIT+GFTI) together. Ranks shown for IITs = JEE Advanced CRL ranks. IIT admission requires: (1) JEE Main top 2.5L → (2) Clear JEE Advanced → (3) JOSAA counselling with JEE Advanced rank. JEE Main rank alone does NOT give IIT admission.
📋 JEE Advanced 2025 Key Facts
Eligible (JEE Main)Top 2,50,000
IIT Seats~17,000
IIT Campuses23
OPEN Cutoff~35-40%
🏛️ IIT Admission Path

1︎ Clear JEE Main - top 2.5 lakh CRL

2︎ Register and appear for JEE Advanced

3︎ Clear JEE Advanced (separate rank)

4︎ Register on JOSAA with JEE Advanced rank

5︎ Fill choices - IIT programs in order

6︎ Seat allotted by JEE Advanced rank

JOSAA Admission Process
1
JEE Main / JEE Advanced
Clear JEE Main (NIT/IIIT/GFTI) or JEE Main + JEE Advanced (IITs). JEE Main is January and April sessions.
Jan-Apr 2026
2
JOSAA Registration
Register on josaa.nic.in. Upload documents: 10th/12th marksheets, category certificate, photo, signature.
Jun 2026
3
Choice Filling and Locking
Fill up to 25,000 institute-program preferences in order. Lock before 5 PM deadline.
Jun 2026
4
Round 1 Seat Allotment
Accept and pay fee (Rs.35,000 OPEN / Rs.15,000 SC/ST) by midnight. Failure = seat cancelled.
Jun-Jul 2026
5
Rounds 2-6 Upgrades
Choose Float to upgrade automatically each round. JOSAA 2025 had 6 rounds.
Jul 2026
6
Document Verification
Visit reporting center with originals + attested copies. Medical fitness test for some programs.
Jul-Aug 2026
7
CSAB Special Round
After JOSAA, CSAB fills vacant NIT/IIIT/GFTI seats. Separate registration required.
Aug 2026
Float vs Freeze: Float = auto-upgrade each round. Freeze = stay put. Always choose Float unless 100% satisfied.
Seat Acceptance Fee: Pay by midnight - no exceptions. Missing deadline = seat cancelled, no refund.
HS Quota: Home State quota for NITs has 15-45% lower cutoffs. Always fill your state NIT with HS quota.
Supernumerary Seats: Female-only seats add ~20% extra capacity above regular seats.
