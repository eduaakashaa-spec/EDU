Each filter on the map represents a different category with its own admission process, fee structure, and academic culture. Fit matters more than label.

Methodology · How to read this

A note on the numbers.

Engineering institution counts are fuzzy because AICTE, UGC, NIRF and state DTE lists rarely agree. Here's how we reconciled them.

Sources. AICTE Approval Process Handbook 2023-24; MoE IoE official list; UGC Deemed Universities list; NIRF 2026 Engineering category; NAAC database (naac.gov.in); TNEA Anna University 2024 participating institutions; state DTE counselling lists. Private counts rounded to nearest 5–10.

What's counted as "engineering". Only institutions offering AICTE-approved B.E./B.Tech programmes. Polytechnics, pure science institutes and B.Sc-only institutions excluded.

TNEA filter. TNEA is a counselling process (not a national category) so the filter highlights only Tamil Nadu. The 571 includes government, aided and self-financing colleges. Tamil Nadu's private colleges are also counted under "Private" — there's deliberate overlap, since most TNEA colleges are private.

Overlap caution. IIT Madras counts under IITs, IoE, NIRF and NAAC. BITS Pilani counts under Deemed, IoE, NIRF and NAAC. Totals across filters will not sum cleanly — by design, since each filter answers a different question.

District drill-down. District figures available for top 12 states (TN, MH, KA, TG, AP, UP, KL, DL, GJ, WB, MP, RJ). Other states show summary only.
