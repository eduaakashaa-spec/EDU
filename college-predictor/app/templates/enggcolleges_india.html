GLOSSARY · KNOW WHAT EACH CATEGORY MEANS

What's the difference between these?

Each filter represents a different category with its own admission process, fee structure and academic culture. Fit matters more than label.

  • 01 · IITs (23 institutes) — Indian Institutes of Technology. Admission via JEE Advanced. Combined ~17,000 BTech seats.
  • 02 · NITs (31 institutes) — National Institutes of Technology. JoSAA using JEE Main. ~24,000 BTech seats.
  • 03 · TNEA (571 colleges) — Tamil Nadu state counselling: government, aided and self-financing colleges. Class 12 cut-off based.
  • 04 · Deemed universities (~125) — UGC-designated autonomous universities. Includes VIT, SRM, BITS, MAHE, Amrita.
  • 05 · Private colleges (~5,200) — Self-financed colleges affiliated to a state technical university. State counselling or management/NRI quota.
  • 06 · Institutions of Eminence (12) — MoE-conferred status with exceptional autonomy and ₹1,000 cr in central support over 5 years.
  • 07 · NIRF Top 25 (25) — MoE National Institutional Ranking Framework 2026 — leading institutes by teaching, research, outcomes, outreach.
  • 08 · NAAC A++ (~28) — National Assessment and Accreditation Council A++ (CGPA 3.51–4.00). The highest peer-reviewed quality grade.
  • 09 · Drill-down (31 states) — District-wise college distribution and notable institutions, state by state.
