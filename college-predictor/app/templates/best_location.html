Section 06 · Pattern Recognition

Six observations from the data that we'd want every parent to know.

Not opinions. Patterns drawn from NIRF rankings, NCRB safety data, employer placement reports and EduAakashaa's own onboarding cohorts.

INS · 01
South India

South India has India's highest engineering ecosystem density.

Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra and Telangana account for ~46% of India's accredited engineering seats — and ~52% of placement-active CSE seats.

INS · 02
Startup density

Bengaluru and Pune dominate startup access.

Across India's DPIIT-recognised startups, Bengaluru + Pune host the largest share — translating into more internship offers per CSE student than any other corridor.

INS · 03
Safety

Tamil Nadu ranks among the safest engineering education ecosystems for NRI families.

NCRB crime-against-student indicators combined with hostel safety self-reporting place TN consistently in the top quartile — particularly for women students.

INS · 04
Semiconductor

Hyderabad is the fastest-growing semiconductor ecosystem.

With ITM-1 wafer fabs and ATMP units anchoring around the city, ECE / VLSI specialisation graduates from Hyderabad-region colleges saw the steepest placement uplift in 2025–26.

INS · 05
Affordable

Coimbatore is the emerging affordable engineering hub.

Cost of living ~38% below Bengaluru; placement parity with Tier-1 South-Indian peers across PSG, CIT and KCT; international airport directly connected to Dubai and Singapore.

INS · 06
For NRI families

Direct international flights matter more than parents expect.

Cities with daily direct flights to Dubai, Singapore and London — Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Coimbatore, Mumbai — show 2× higher NRI student retention compared to single-stop cities.
