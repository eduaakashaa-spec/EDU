COMPARE QUALIFICATIONS

Three World-Class Credentials

Each qualification opens unique career doors. Here's a deep dive into what sets them apart.

ACCA
ACCA — Association of Chartered Certified Accountants
  • Exams: 13 papers across 3 levels
  • Duration: 2–3 years (fast track available)
  • Pass Rate: 40–55% per paper
  • Recognition: 180+ countries globally
  • Best For: Accounting, audit, taxation, advisory
  • Starting Salary: ₹4–8 LPA (India), £25–35K (UK)
CFA
CFA — Chartered Financial Analyst
  • Exams: 3 levels (increasingly difficult)
  • Duration: 2.5–4 years typical
  • Pass Rate: Level I ~38%, Level III ~52%
  • Recognition: Gold standard in investment management
  • Best For: Portfolio management, equity research, risk
  • Starting Salary: ₹6–12 LPA (India), $65–90K (USA)
CA
CA — Chartered Accountant (ICAI)
  • Exams: Foundation + Intermediate + Final
  • Duration: 4–5 years (including articleship)
  • Pass Rate: 5–15% (one of the toughest globally)
  • Recognition: Highest prestige in India
  • Best For: Tax, audit, corporate finance, compliance
  • Starting Salary: ₹7–12 LPA, top firms ₹15–25 LPA

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pursue ACCA and CFA together?
Yes, many professionals hold both. ACCA covers accounting depth while CFA covers investment expertise. However, pursuing them simultaneously is demanding — most complete one first, then add the other.
Is CA valid outside India?
ICAI has Mutual Recognition Agreements (MRAs) with CA bodies in the UK, Canada, Australia, and others. However, you typically need to pass additional exams for local practice rights.
Which has the best ROI?
CA offers the best ROI in India due to low cost and high starting salaries. CFA has excellent ROI in investment banking/asset management globally. ACCA offers the best ROI for those wanting to work internationally.
Can I start after 12th?
CA Foundation can be attempted after 12th. ACCA accepts students after 12th with certain conditions. CFA requires a bachelor's degree or being in the final year of a bachelor's program.
What about AI impact on these roles?
Routine bookkeeping is being automated, but advisory, strategic analysis, and audit judgment roles are growing. All three qualifications are adapting curricula to include data analytics, AI, and technology modules.
