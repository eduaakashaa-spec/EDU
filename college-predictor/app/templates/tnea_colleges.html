About this view

All TNEA colleges

All 571 colleges admit through TNEA — the Anna University-run counselling that uses Class 12 PCM marks (out of 200) as the cut-off. Chennai, Coimbatore, Kanchipuram, Chengalpattu and Tiruchirappalli together host nearly half of all colleges.

TNEA process · Five steps

How does TNEA actually work?

From registration to seat allotment, TNEA runs as a centralised process between May and August. Here's the timeline at a glance.

Step 01

Registration

Apply online at tneaonline.org with Class 12 marksheet, community certificate and ₹500–600 fee. Window opens early May.

Step 02

Random number

Anna University assigns a random number for tie-breaking when two candidates share the same cut-off score.

Step 03

Rank list

Cut-off + random number determine the overall rank. Separate rank lists published for OC, BC, BCM, MBC, SC, SCA, ST.

Step 04

Choice filling

Candidates fill college-branch preferences (up to 75) in order. Higher rank → earlier counselling slot.

Step 05

Allotment

Seats allotted in three rounds (Aug–Sep) based on rank, choice order and availability. Confirm by paying the seat fee.
