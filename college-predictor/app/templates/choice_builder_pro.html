    After Allotment — Freeze, Float or Slide?

    Once Round I allots you a seat, DASA asks for your willingness. These three choices decide what happens in the next round. Below the explainer, we map your predicted seat to the exact choices you could still move up to.

    🔒
    FREEZE
    Accept the allotted seat as final. You exit all further rounds — the seat is locked in and can't change. Pick this when your allotted seat is genuinely what you want.
    Result: seat confirmed, no upgrade, no risk.
    🌊
    FLOAT
    Keep the current seat but stay in the running for any higher-preference choice across all institutes. If one opens in the next round you're upgraded; if not, you keep the current seat.
    Result: current seat safe, may upgrade to a better preference anywhere.
    ➡️
    SLIDE
    Keep the current seat but stay in the running only for higher preferences within the same institute. Safer than Float — you'll never be moved to a different campus.
    Result: current seat safe, may upgrade to a better branch in the same college.
