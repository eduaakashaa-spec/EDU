IIT Admission Clarity
Why does this tool show IIT data with JEE Main ranks?
JOSAA processes admissions using both JEE Main (for NIT/IIIT/GFTI) and JEE Advanced (for IITs) under one platform. The opening/closing ranks shown for IITs in JOSAA data correspond to JEE Advanced ranks (CRL) — not JEE Main ranks. The top-2.5 lakh JEE Main qualifiers appear in JOSAA, and those who also cleared JEE Advanced get IIT seats. This tool displays those rank ranges for reference, but remember: IIT admission requires JEE Advanced clearance.

JOSAA Admission Process
1
JEE Main / JEE Advanced
Qualify JEE Main (for NIT/IIIT/GFTI) or both JEE Main + JEE Advanced (for IITs). JEE Main is conducted by NTA in January and April sessions.
Jan–Apr 2026
2
JOSAA Registration
Register on josaa.nic.in. Upload documents: 10th/12th marksheets, category certificate, photo, signature. One account per candidate.
Jun 2026 (Expected)
3
Choice Filling & Locking
Fill up to 25,000 institute-program preferences in order. Order matters! Lock choices before deadline. Strategy: fill ALL possible options.
Jun 2026
4
Round 1 Seat Allotment
Based on rank, category & filled choices. Accept & pay seat acceptance fee (₹35,000 for OPEN, ₹15,000 for SC/ST). Respond within deadline.
Jun–Jul 2026
5
Rounds 2–6 (Upgrades)
If you accept seat, you can participate in upgrade rounds automatically. JOSAA 2025 had 6 rounds. Final allotment comes after Round 6.
Jul 2026
6
Document Verification
Visit reporting center (allotted institute or designated center). Carry originals + attested copies. Medical fitness test required for some programs.
Jul–Aug 2026
7
CSAB Special Round
After JOSAA, CSAB conducts special rounds for vacant NIT/IIIT/GFTI seats. Separate registration required. Good for last-minute improvements.
Aug 2026
⚠️
Critical Rules to Know
⚠️ Floating vs Freeze: Choose "Float" to upgrade automatically, "Freeze" to stay. Never choose "Slide" unless you understand it means same institute, better branch.
⚠️ Seat Acceptance Fee: Paid once and adjusted in tuition. Not paying = seat cancelled. Pay via net banking/UPI by midnight deadline.
📘 HS Quota: Home State quota for NITs offers significantly lower cutoffs (15–40% lower). Only your state's NIT allows HS quota.
📘 Supernumerary Seats: Female-only seats are over and above regular seats. Adds 20% extra seats for girls in most programs.
