FOUNDATIONS · DASA & CIWG

Two paths into India's NITs that look identical — but aren't.

Most families discover the difference between DASA and CIWG only after they've started filling forms. Here's the plain-English version, before you commit to anything.

🎓
DASA — Direct Admission of Students Abroad
The umbrella scheme run on behalf of the Ministry of Education by a rotating coordinating NIT (NIT Calicut for the 2026 cycle, expected). It opens seats at NITs, IIITs, SPAs and other CFTIs to NRIs, OCI/PIO and foreign nationals. Admission is based solely on JEE Main 2026 All India Rank — SAT Subject Tests are permanently discontinued.
🪪
CIWG — Children of Indian Workers in Gulf
A sub-category inside DASA carved out for children of Indian-passport-holding workers in the six GCC countries (UAE, KSA, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait). The 2025 cycle filled 215 programs here. Since 2021, CIWG admissions are ranked on the same JEE Main AIR — not SAT.
DASA eligibility
Valid JEE Main 2026 rank + Class XII (PCM, min. 75% aggregate or 7.5 CGPA).
DASA application
Online at dasanit.org; registration fee USD 300 (non-refundable).
DASA (NRI route)
Class XI & XII completed abroad (min. 2 years of study in the last 8).
CIWG tuition (2025)
₹62,500 per semester — roughly half the Non-CIWG DASA seat fee.
CIWG proof
Parent's GCC employment + Indian passport for both parent & child must be documented.
CIWG merit
Same JEE Main rank pool, but a separate merit list & closing ranks — usually more accessible than Non-CIWG.

PROCESS · 6 STEPS · DASANIT.ORG The DASA 2026 admission journey, JEE Main first

Since 2021 the Ministry of Education has made JEE Main the sole entrance exam for DASA and CIWG. SAT Subject Tests are permanently discontinued. Here is the exact 2026 sequence — expected dates from official communication so far; the final calendar is confirmed in the DASA 2026 brochure on dasanit.org.

1
Appear for JEE Main 2026
B.E./B.Tech → Paper 1. B.Arch → Paper 2A. B.Plan → Paper 2B. JEE Main runs twice (January & April) by NTA; the better of the two scores is used. NRIs can write it from designated overseas centres including the UAE.
2
Confirm eligibility
Born on or after 1 Oct 2001. Class XII passed (or appearing 2026) with PCM & min. 75% aggregate (7.5 CGPA), 70% / 7.0 for SC/ST. NRIs: Class XI & XII abroad (min. 2 years of foreign schooling in the last 8). CIWG candidates additionally need GCC-employment proof for their parent.
3
Register on dasanit.org
Application window opens around late April / early May 2026 and stays open ~30 days. Use your JEE Main 2026 application number as username; activate via the email link. The coordinating institute for 2026 is expected to be NIT Calicut.
4
Documents & fee payment
Upload passport, JEE Main scorecard, Class X & XII marksheets, residence/study proof, and (for CIWG) parent's GCC visa + employer letter. Pay the non-refundable USD 300 fee via SWIFT / NEFT / card / UPI. Names must match exactly across passport, JEE Main and DASA form.
5
Choice filling & locking
Pick institute×branch combinations in priority order from the DASA 2026 seat matrix — fill 40+ choices. Lock before the deadline; unlocked choices are not considered. A mock allotment runs before Round 1 so you can revise.
6
3 rounds → reporting
Rounds 1, 2 & 3 run late June through July. After each allotment choose Accept (locks the seat), Accept & Upgrade (stays in the running for a higher choice), or Decline (exits). Pay first-semester tuition — ₹62,500 for CIWG, equivalent USD for Non-CIWG — and report by late July / early August 2026.
FAQ · 2026 CYCLE

Questions Gulf-NRI families ask us every week.

Straight, fact-checked answers from the official DASA 2026 brochure, dasanit.org and NTA circulars. If yours isn't here, write to info@eduaakashaa.com.

Do I still need to write the SAT for DASA or CIWG in 2026?
No. The Ministry of Education made JEE Main mandatory for all DASA/CIWG admissions in 2021, and the College Board has since permanently discontinued SAT Subject Tests worldwide. JEE Main 2026 is the sole entrance exam for DASA UG 2026 admissions to NITs, IIITs, SPAs and other CFTIs — for NRIs, OCI/PIO, foreign nationals and CIWG candidates alike. The SAT plays no role.
Can my child write JEE Main 2026 from Dubai / Doha / Riyadh?
Yes. NTA conducts JEE Main twice a year (January & April) and operates designated overseas centres in the GCC including the UAE (Dubai & Abu Dhabi), Bahrain (Manama), Kuwait, Oman (Muscat), Qatar (Doha) and Saudi Arabia (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam), plus Kathmandu, Singapore, Colombo, Lagos and others. NRI candidates register on jeemain.nta.nic.in and pick the nearest overseas centre. The better of the two session scores is used for the DASA merit list.
Is my child eligible for CIWG, or only Non-CIWG DASA?
CIWG eligibility hinges on three things: (1) your child holds an Indian passport, (2) you (the parent) hold an Indian passport and are currently employed in one of the six GCC countries — UAE, KSA, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, (3) your child completed Class XI and XII abroad (min. 2 years of foreign schooling in the last 8). If any of these is missing, the only DASA route open is Non-CIWG (NRI Quota), which has significantly higher fees but typically slightly more competitive closing ranks.
How is my DASA / CIWG merit rank decided in 2026?
By your JEE Main 2026 All India Rank — nothing else. SAT Subject Tests are permanently discontinued worldwide. DASA produces two separate ranked lists each year (CIWG and Non-CIWG / NRI Quota), but both are drawn from the same JEE Main score pool. The closing ranks shown across this page are JEE Main AIRs from the 2025 cycle.
What's the real fee difference between CIWG and Non-CIWG?
For the 2025 cycle: CIWG seats at NITs were ₹62,500 per semester (approx. ₹1.25 lakh per year). Non-CIWG / DASA NRI seats were billed in USD — roughly USD 4,000 per semester for SAARC candidates and ~USD 8,000 per semester for non-SAARC at NITs (lower at SPAs & some IIITs). Add hostel and one-time admission charges. Both categories pay the same USD 300 non-refundable application fee. Confirm 2026 figures on dasanit.org before transferring funds.
My CIWG rank is 25,000 — what can I realistically expect?
At a JEE Main 2025 AIR of 25,000 in the CIWG list, you'd be competitive for a wide spread of NIT branches in core engineering (Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, EEE) at second-tier NITs, plus several IIITs in CSE-adjacent branches. CSE at top NITs like Trichy or Surathkal will be very tight. The predictor will show your exact Safe, Moderate and Reach options — try it with your actual JEE Main rank.
Is a DASA NIT seat treated equal to a JEE NIT seat after graduation?
Yes. The degree certificate is identical — same institute, same syllabus, same campus, same placement cell. Recruiters do not distinguish between DASA and JEE entrants on the degree itself. The only operational difference is the fee structure during the four (or five) years of study.
How many choices should I fill, and in what order?
Fill at least 40 choices. The rule we recommend: top-load with your Reach options (programs slightly above your rank — the system follows your order strictly, so don't fill in fear of being auto-allotted something unwanted), then Moderate, then Safe. Skipping the safe ones is a common mistake — a single round of bad luck and the seat slips. The predictor splits your options exactly along these lines.
Does the data on this page reflect 2026 admissions?
The closing ranks here are JEE Main 2025 All India Ranks from the actual 2025 DASA cycle — not projections. The 2026 ranks will be published by the coordinating institute (NIT Calicut, expected) on dasanit.org after each round. Year-on-year closing ranks historically shift 5–15% in either direction; treat the 2025 numbers as a strong baseline, not a guarantee.
