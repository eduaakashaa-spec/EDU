Questions · For students & parents

The things parents ask first.

Q·01 · For parents
Do we pay anything to apply?

No. The application is completely free and carries no obligation. You only receive an invoice — and only pay — after we’ve reviewed the profile and approved the right path. There is no upfront payment at any point.

Q·02 · For parents
How is this different from coaching?

Coaching teaches the answers for an exam. Mentorship decides which questions to ask — which stream, which colleges, which exams, and in what order. Both matter, but only the decision determines where a student actually ends up. EduAakashaa is the decision layer, delivered as a written, evidence-based plan.

Q·03 · For students
Which exams do you guide for?

Over twelve pathways under one mentor — including JOSAA, TNEA, DASA, NATA, NID, NEET, CIWG, IIIT, VITEEE, AMRITA, CAT and MCA admissions. You don’t juggle multiple consultants; one mentor coordinates the whole picture.

Q·04 · For parents
Is the mentoring online or in person?

All mentoring is fully online — over video calls, phone, WhatsApp and email — so a student anywhere in India or abroad gets the same senior mentor without travel. You’ll have scheduled sessions plus message support through the year. Mentoring hours are Monday to Friday 9:00–22:00 and Saturday & Sunday 9:00–16:00 (IST).
