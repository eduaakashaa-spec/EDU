The full DASA 2026 counselling calendar

Every official milestone from the DASA 2026 Information Brochure, in sequence. Orange markers are the deadlines you must not miss. All times are in IST.

Tue, 28 Jul 2026 · 10:00Registration opens

Registration, DEF payment, document upload & choice filling START

Display of vacancies after JoSAA rounds. Registration, payment of DASA Enrolment Fee (DEF) and document uploading begin. Choices can be filled only after successful document verification.

Fri, 31 Jul 2026 · 10:00Mock

Display of mock seat allocation

Based on choices filled by candidates as on Thu, 30 Jul 2026, 17:00 hrs. Use this to refine and re-order your choices.

Mon, 03 Aug 2026 · 10:00Deadline

Registration, DEF payment & document upload END

Last opportunity to complete registration, pay the enrolment fee and upload all required documents (Annexure 5). Late submissions are not accepted.

Tue, 04 Aug 2026 · 21:00Deadline

Respond to verification queries & pay balance DEF

Last day to respond to the Document Verifying Officer's queries and to pay balance DEF, if applicable. Payment-issue resolution closes 17:00. Unresolved queries lead to cancellation of candidature.

Wed, 05 Aug 2026 · 14:00Choice locking

Choice filling & locking END (auto-lock)

The system auto-locks your last saved choices at 14:00. Data reconciliation for eligible candidates follows at 17:00. Lock your choices early to be safe.

Thu, 06 Aug 2026 · 17:00Round-I

Round-I seat allotment result

First seat allotment is displayed on the portal. Allotted candidates must report online and exercise their willingness option.

06 Aug 17:00 → 10 Aug 17:00Willingness

Exercise willingness (online reporting)

Choose one: FREEZE, SLIDE, FLOAT, SURRENDER, WITHDRAW or EXIT. No response by the deadline is treated as the default option — act on time.

Wed, 12 Aug 2026 · 17:00Round-II · Final

Round-II seat allotment result

The final DASA round. All allotments in Round-II are presumed to be accepted. Prepare for physical reporting at your allotted institute.

Thu, 13 Aug → Tue, 18 Aug 2026Reporting

Physical reporting & balance fee payment

Tentative window for physical reporting at the allotted institute and payment of balance fees. Check your allotted institute's website for exact dates and class commencement.

How EduAakashaa walks you through it

The stages of the DASA seat-allocation process, in the order they happen. Bring your JEE (Main) 2026 credentials and documents.

01

Registration & eligibility

Login with JEE (Main) 2026 number, confirm nationality / CIWG status, and register on the NIC seat-allocation portal.

02

Fee payment (DEF)

Pay the DASA Enrolment Fee — $2300 for CIWG, $4300 for Non-CIWG — via SWIFT / e-Payment / NEFT / RTGS.

03

Document upload & verification

Upload all required documents (Annexure 5) and respond promptly to any verifying-officer queries to avoid cancellation.

04

Choice filling & locking

Strategise institute/branch choices using the mock allotment, then lock before the 05 Aug deadline — or risk auto-lock.

05

Rounds & willingness

Review Round-I & Round-II allotments and choose Freeze / Slide / Float / Surrender / Withdraw / Exit on time.

06

Reporting & admission

Complete physical reporting and balance-fee payment at your allotted institute within the 13–18 Aug window.

Quick answers · For students & parents

Questions we hear most often

Q·01 · For students

Who qualifies for the CIWG category?

+

NRI candidates whose either or both parents are working — or have worked till any date in 2026 — in a Gulf country (UAE, Bahrain, Iraq, Iran, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia). CIWG seats are 5% of DASA-GEN seats; vacant CIWG seats convert to Non-CIWG at Round-II. Supporting documents are listed in Annexure 5 (including the parent's Gulf passport copy).

Q·02 · For parents

How many rounds are there, and which is final?

+

DASA 2026 conducts Round-I (result on 06 Aug) and Round-II (result on 12 Aug). Round-II is the final round — all allotments in it are presumed accepted. After Round-I you exercise a willingness option; if you do nothing, the default applies.

Q·03 · For students

What if I miss the choice-locking deadline?

+

The system auto-locks your last saved choices at 14:00 on 05 Aug 2026. Unlocked choices that were never saved are not considered. Save frequently and lock early — don't rely on the auto-lock.

Q·04 · For parents

Is the registration fee refundable?

+

No. The US$ 300 registration fee is non-refundable. Tuition fee refund rules are governed by Annexure 6 of the brochure. The DEF is paid once via SWIFT / e-Payment / NEFT / RTGS.

Q·05 · For students

I already secured a seat in JoSAA — can I still join DASA?

+

Yes, as a fresh candidate after paying the DEF, provided you meet DASA eligibility. If a new seat is allotted in DASA, your retained JoSAA seat is automatically cancelled. Note: securing an IIT seat in JoSAA prevents you from appearing for JEE (Advanced) 2027.
