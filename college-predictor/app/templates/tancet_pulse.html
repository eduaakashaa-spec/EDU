HOW IT WORKS

The MicroLearn Approach

01
Daily Micro-Sessions (15 min)
Short, focused practice sessions that fit into any schedule. Questions adapt to student's current level.
02
Confidence Tracking
Students rate their confidence on each topic. The system identifies gaps between confidence and actual performance.
03
Adaptive Difficulty
Questions automatically adjust difficulty based on performance. Struggling students get easier problems; advanced students get challenges.
04
Weekly Reports
Comprehensive analytics sent to students, parents, and teachers. Track improvement trends, weak areas, and time spent.
