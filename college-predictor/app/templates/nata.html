NATA 2026 Step-by-Step

The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) is conducted by the Council of Architecture (CoA).

1
Online Registration
Register at nata.in with email & mobile. Upload photo / signature. Fee: General ₹1,750 · SC/ST ₹1,250.
2
Eligibility Check
10+2 with Physics & Maths (50% aggregate) or 10+3 Diploma with Mathematics. Min age 17 by Jul 31, 2026.
3
Exam Pattern
3-hour exam, 200 marks. Part A: Drawing & Composition (offline, 80). Part B: MCQ/NCQ (online, 120). No negative marking.
4
Attempt & Score
Up to 3 attempts in Phase 1. Best score is considered. Qualifying cutoff ≈60/200. Top colleges need 120–145+.
5
Admit Card
Available Tuesday for Friday exams, Wednesday for Saturday exams. Carry printed copy + valid photo ID.
6
Counselling & Admission
Colleges conduct their own counselling. Apply to 450+ CoA-recognized institutes across India.

INTEREST TEST

Is Architecture Right For You?

  • Enjoy sketching, drawing, visualising 3-D objects from 2-D references.
  • Strong observation skills — notice proportion, scale, light & shadow.
  • Comfortable working long hours on design studio projects.
  • Interest in history, culture & sustainability alongside engineering logic.
  • Patience & attention to detail — CAD, physical models, concept boards.
