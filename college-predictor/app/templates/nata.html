NATA 2026 Exam Process

Conducted by the Council of Architecture (CoA) for B.Arch admission at 450+ recognised institutions across India.

01

Online Registration

Register at nata.in. Upload photo (4–100 KB) & signature (1–30 KB) in JPG. Fee: General/OBC ₹1,750 | SC/ST/EWS/PwD ₹1,250 | Transgender ₹1,000. Print confirmation page.

02

Eligibility Check

10+2 with Physics & Mathematics (compulsory) + Chemistry/Biology/CS/IT. Or 10+3 Diploma with Maths. Min 45% aggregate (40% SC/ST/OBC). Age: 17+ by 31 July 2026.

03

Exam Pattern

200 marks, 3 hrs. Part A: Drawing & Composition (offline, 3 Qs, 80 marks, 90 min). Part B: MCQ & NCQ (computer-based, 50 Qs, 120 marks, 90 min). No negative marking.

04

Multiple Attempts

Max 3 attempts (2 in Phase 1, 1 in Phase 2). Best of 2 scores considered. For 3 attempts, valid score = average of best two. Not mandatory to take all attempts.

05

Admit Card & Results

Download from nata.in. Available Tue (for Fri exam) / Wed (for Sat exam). Carry printed copy + photo ID. Results within 7–10 days. Save scorecard for admissions.

06

Counselling & Admission

CoA does not conduct centralised counselling. Apply directly to colleges or state counselling. Each college sets own cutoff marks. Top colleges typically need 120–145+ marks.

Decision Making · Insights

Key Insights for Students & Parents

Critical factors before choosing your path.

🎯

Follow Aptitude, Not Trends

If drawing excites you but JEE math feels like a struggle, don’t force-fit into engineering just because it’s popular.

💰

ROI & Salary

Engineering has wider starting salary range, but Architecture from top NIRF colleges offers strong and comparable ROI.

📚

5 Years vs 4 Years

B.Arch’s extra year includes mandatory internship and thesis, giving real-world experience before graduation.

🌎

Global Opportunities

Architects can pursue RIBA (UK) and AIA (USA) certifications. Both paths offer strong international prospects.

🚀

Crossover Fields

Computational design, sustainable architecture, smart cities, and BIM blend both Architecture and Engineering skills.

💡

Try Before You Decide

Sketch a room design vs solve a math puzzle. Whichever excites you more reveals your true calling.
