ADMISSION ROUTES

Three Ways In

Each top IIIT has a different NRI admission process. Here's how they work.

A
Route A — IIIT Hyderabad
SAT-based admission. Does NOT use JEE Main. Apply directly through IIIT-H portal with SAT scores. Separate process from DASA.
B
Route B — IIIT Bangalore
Uses JEE Main OR own entrance portal. IIIT-B manages its own admission for NRI/international students. Check iiitb.ac.in for updates.
C
Route C — All Other IIITs
JEE Main based, through dasanit.org (DASA portal). Includes IIIT Allahabad, IIIT Delhi, IIIT Pune, and all other IIITs in the DASA scheme.
A
ROUTE A IIIT Hyderabad — SAT Based

IIIT Hyderabad is India's #1 ranked IIIT (NIRF #47 overall, #3 among IIITs). It conducts its own NRI admission using SAT scores — completely independent of JEE Main and DASA.

01
Register on IIIT-H Admissions Portal
Visit admissions.iiit.ac.in and create an account. Select NRI/NRI-sponsored category.
02
Submit SAT Scores
Upload official SAT score report. IIIT-H uses SAT Math + Evidence-Based Reading scores for ranking.
03
Upload Documents
NRI proof (passport, visa, employment letter), 10+2 marksheet, SAT scorecard, photographs.
04
Merit List & Counselling
IIIT-H publishes merit list based on SAT scores. Online counselling with branch preference filling.
05
Seat Acceptance & Fee Payment
Accept allocated seat and pay first-year fees (approx. ₹10–12 lakh for NRI category).
06
Report to Campus
Document verification and campus registration at IIIT-H, Gachibowli, Hyderabad.
