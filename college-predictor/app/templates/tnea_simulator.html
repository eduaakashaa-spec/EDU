PRIMER

What is TNEA Counselling?

TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) is the centralised counselling for B.E./B.Tech admissions in TN colleges. Your cutoff (Maths + Physics/2 + Chemistry/2 = max 200) determines your rank and eligibility.

STRATEGY

Do's and Don'ts

✅ Do

  • Fill 200+ choices for maximum safety
  • Research placements & infrastructure
  • Mix safe, moderate & ambitious choices
  • Consider branch future scope & interest
  • Keep certificates and documents ready
  • Attend ALL counselling rounds
  • Consult EduAakashaA experts

❌ Don't

  • Don't fill only top colleges
  • Don't ignore branch preference
  • Don't rely only on others' opinions
  • Don't skip any counselling round
  • Don't choose based on peer pressure
  • Don't ignore location & hostel
  • Don't rush — research carefully
