Framework · Built on Research Grounded in Physics Education Research, not guesswork.

We don't grade you on what you got wrong. We diagnose why you got it wrong — and what cognitive pattern produced the error.

01
Mental models

Diagnose how you internally picture motion, force, and energy. Reveals whether your reasoning matches Newtonian or Aristotelian models.

02
Misconception traps

30% of items are research-validated distractors. Selecting one identifies a specific conceptual confusion to target.

03
Confidence mapping

You rate your certainty on every answer (1–5). Confidence × correctness reveals mastery, gaps, and dangerous misconceptions.

04
Skill profile

Six-skill tagging — conceptual, visual, mathematical, multi-step, misconception, and transfer — produces your learner type (A/B/C/D).
