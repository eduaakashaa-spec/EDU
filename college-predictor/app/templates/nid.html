ADMISSION PROCESS

Two-stage DAT: Prelims → Mains → Counselling

1
Online Registration
Create an account at admissions.nid.edu. Upload photo, signature & category certificates.
2
Pay Application Fee
General/OBC/EWS: ₹3,000 · SC/ST/PwD: ₹1,500 · Overseas: ₹5,000.
3
DAT Prelims
3-hour written test · MCQ + subjective. Tests design aptitude, creativity, visualisation, observation & GK.
4
Prelims Result
Only 1.5× candidates (of total seats) shortlisted for Mains. Prelims score is NOT counted in final merit.
5
DAT Mains — Studio Test
Sketching, model-making, design exercises. M.Des: studio test + interview.
6
Final Merit
Based entirely on Mains performance. Category-wise merit lists released.
7
Counselling
June 2026 · Submit campus preferences, verify documents, pay token fee.
8
Report to Campus
Submit original documents, pay semester fees and begin your design journey.

COMPARE

Engineering (B.Tech) vs NID (B.Des)

⚙️ Engineering (B.Tech/B.E.)

  • 4-year technical degree via JEE / state CETs
  • Focus on Maths, Physics, Computer Science
  • Career: Software, Core Engg, Research
  • Avg package top IITs: ₹15–25 LPA
  • Analytical & logical thinking

🎨 NID Design (B.Des)

  • 4-year degree via NID DAT
  • Focus on creativity, visual thinking, aptitude
  • Career: UX/UI, Product, Animation, Branding
  • Avg NID Ahmedabad: ₹17–30 LPA
  • Creative & visual thinking

OVERSEAS / NRI

International Applicants Guide

  • Overseas category: 15% supernumerary seats in B.Des and M.Des. For foreign passport holders with PIO / OCI.
  • NRI with Indian passport: NOT eligible for Overseas category. Must apply as Indian nationals.
  • OCI / PIO: Apply only under Overseas category. Pay foreign-citizen fees.
  • Student visa: Mandatory for Overseas candidates before joining.
