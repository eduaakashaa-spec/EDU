PATHWAYS

All Admission Pathways at Amrita

Amrita offers multiple entrance exams and routes across engineering, life sciences, law and medical.

🔧
AEEE — Engineering
B.Tech entrance across 8 campuses. CBT, 100 MCQs / 300 marks / 150 mins. 70% seats via AEEE, 30% via JEE Main. 19 specializations. Two phases (Jan–Feb & Apr).
🧬
AEEL — Life Sciences
Nursing, Pharmacy, Agriculture, Food Science & Allied Health Sciences. CBT, 80 Qs / 120 mins. Physics, Chemistry, Biology & English.
⚖️
A-CLAT — Law
Integrated 5-year B.A. LL.B. and B.B.A. LL.B. at Coimbatore. Also accepts CLAT / AILET. Annual fee ~₹2.25 L + ₹20,000 deposit.
📊
JEE Main Route
30% of B.Tech seats via JEE Main percentile. Apply through the same AEEE portal, no additional fee.
🌍
NRI / OCI / PIO
Direct admission without AEEE for foreign passport holders of Indian origin. SAT accepted. Fee ~USD 7,000/year for B.Tech.
🏥
NEET Route
MBBS / BDS / Nursing at Kochi & Faridabad via NEET. Amrita Hospital — a premier super-specialty institution.
📅

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can NRI students get direct admission without AEEE?
Yes. Foreign passport holders of Indian origin (OCI/PIO) can apply under the NRI quota and do not need AEEE. SAT is accepted as an alternative.
What is the NRI fee structure for B.Tech?
Approximately USD 7,000 per year across all engineering campuses. Separate from the Indian fee slabs.
Do NRI students need SAT scores?
SAT is one of several accepted options. Merit in Class 12 + SAT/AEEE scores are considered for NRI allotment.
What is CSAP counselling?
Amrita's centralized seat allotment process. After Phase 2 results, register for CSAP, fill campus & branch preferences, and pay fees online.
Can I apply through both AEEE and JEE Main?
Yes — and you should. Best of the two scores is considered. No additional fee for opting into the JEE Main route.
How are scholarships maintained after admission?
Slab-1 scholarship needs 8.0+ CGPA; Slab-2 needs 7.5+ CGPA with 75% attendance each semester.
